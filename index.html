Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random Target Score at the start of the game.
Each of the four crystals have been randomly assigned a value.
Clicking on each crystal adds a specific amount of points to your Total Score.
You win the game by matching your Total Score to the Target Score.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it.
Each time the game restarts, the values of each crystal will also be re-randomized.
